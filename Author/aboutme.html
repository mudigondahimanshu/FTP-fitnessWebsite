This Site

FTP: Feel The Pump is a hand-built static site — no frameworks, just HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript, powered under the hood by classic data structures:

  • Trie — prefix search for finding exercises fast
  • Priority queue — the to-do list always surfaces what matters most
  • LRU cache — remembers your recently viewed pages
