How the targets are calculated

  • Calories: body weight (kg) × 26
  • Protein: body weight (kg) × 2
  • Fat: calories ÷ 30
  • Fiber: calories × 0.014
  • Carbs: (calories − protein × 4 − fat × 9) ÷ 4
  • Water: body weight (kg) ÷ 30

Always track the carbohydrates you take in to keep tabs on your progress — estimates only, not medical advice.
