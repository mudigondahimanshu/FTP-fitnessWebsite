Fat Loss Diet Plan

Built for a moderate activity level (workouts 3–5 times a week), this plan keeps you in a steady calorie deficit while protecting muscle — clean meals, high protein and simple habits.

Daily Meal Plan

What to Eat, Meal by Meal

Early Morning

Wake-Up Fuel

  • 1 glass warm water with lemon
  • 5–6 soaked almonds + 2 walnuts
Breakfast

Two Options

  • Option 1: 3 egg whites + 1 whole egg bhurji, 1 multigrain toast or 1 small phulka, 1 cup green tea or black coffee (no sugar)
  • Option 2: Oats porridge with low-fat milk + 1 boiled egg, ½ banana (optional)
Mid-Morning Snack

Light & Fresh

  • 1 fruit (apple, orange, guava or papaya)
  • Green tea (optional)
Lunch

Two Options

  • Option 1: Grilled chicken breast (100–120 g), 1 cup brown rice or 2 rotis, mixed vegetable sabzi, green salad
  • Option 2: 1 cup dal + 2 boiled eggs + 1 chapati, vegetable salad, ½ cup low-fat curd
Evening Snack

Protein Top-Up

  • Green tea or black coffee (no sugar)
  • 1 boiled egg, roasted chana (30 g) or 1 whey protein shake
Dinner

Two Options

  • Option 1: Grilled fish or chicken (100 g), sautéed veggies, 1 small roti or ½ cup cooked quinoa
  • Option 2: Egg-white omelette with veggies + clear soup
Post-Dinner

If Hungry

  • 1 glass warm turmeric milk or chamomile tea
Beyond the Plate

Weekly Workout Plan & Smart Tips

Weekly Workout Plan

  • Strength training: 4–5x/week (45 minutes)
  • Cardio: 30–45 minutes of walking, cycling or HIIT
  • Steps goal: 8,000–10,000 daily

Smart Tips

  • Hydration: 2.5–3 litres of water per day
  • Avoid fried food, refined carbs, sugar and packaged foods
  • Meal prep helps you stick to portions and macros
  • One cheat meal per week is fine — not cheat days
  • Sleep 7–8 hours a night for fat loss and recovery

Expected Weight Loss

0.7–1 kg per week — roughly 10–12 kg in 3 months if followed consistently.
