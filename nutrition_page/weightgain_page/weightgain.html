Weight Gain Diet Plan

Healthy weight gain through an increased calorie intake of roughly 2,700–2,800 kcal per day, with a balanced focus on muscle gain and nutrition — not junk calories.

General Guidelines

Daily Meal Plan

Six Meals to Grow On

Early Morning · 7:00 AM

Start Strong

  • 1 banana
  • 5 soaked almonds + 2 dates
  • 1 glass full-fat milk
Breakfast · 8:30–9:00 AM

Two Options

  • Option 1: 2-egg omelette with veggies + 2 whole wheat toasts + 1 glass mango shake or milkshake
  • Option 2: 2–3 paneer parathas with ghee + curd
Mid-Morning · 11:00–11:30 AM

Two Options

  • Option 1: Peanut butter sandwich + coconut water
  • Option 2: Handful of peanuts + banana shake
Lunch · 1:30–2:00 PM

Two Options

  • Option 1 — Chicken meal: 1 cup white rice + 150 g grilled or spicy chicken breast + 1 chapati with ghee, plus salad, curd and a cooked vegetable
  • Option 2 — Veg meal: 1 cup rice + 1–2 chapatis, 1 cup dal, rajma or chole, 1 bowl vegetable curry (potato, paneer, etc.), salad + 1 tsp ghee
Evening Snack · 5:00–6:00 PM

Calorie Boost

  • High-calorie smoothie (milk + banana + peanut butter + oats + honey)
  • OR chicken sandwich or boiled eggs + toast + 1 fruit
Dinner · 8:00–8:30 PM

Three Options

  • Option 1 — Fish meal: 150 g grilled or curried fish + 1–2 chapatis + cooked veggies + salad
  • Option 2 — Chicken meal: 1 chapati + small bowl of rice + chicken curry (150 g) + veg + ghee + salad
  • Option 3 — Paneer meal: 150 g paneer + 2 chapatis + cooked veggies + salad
Bedtime · 10:00 PM

Wind Down

  • 1 glass warm milk with honey or turmeric
  • Optional: a handful of nuts
