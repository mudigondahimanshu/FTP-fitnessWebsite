Shoulder Breakdown

Build wide, round delts using presses and raises that hit every angle of the shoulder — front, middle and rear deltoids.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Front Deltoids

Beginner
  • Front Raises
  • Cable Front Raises
  • Plate Raises
  • Smith Machine Shoulder Press
Intermediate
  • Military Press
  • Arnold Press
  • Push Press
  • Landmine Press

Middle Deltoids

Beginner
  • Lateral Raises
  • Cable Lateral Raises
  • Machine Lateral Raises
  • Seated Dumbbell Press
Intermediate
  • Upright Rows
  • Leaning Lateral Raises
  • W-Press
  • Standing Barbell Press

Rear Deltoids

Beginner
  • Face Pulls
  • Rear Delt Flyes
  • Reverse Pec Deck
  • Bent-Over Lateral Raises
  • Cable Rear Delt Flyes
Intermediate
  • Band Pull-Aparts
  • Seated Rear Delt Rows
  • Prone Rear Delt Raises
