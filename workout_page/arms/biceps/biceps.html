Arms

Biceps Breakdown

Target your biceps with curls, hammer curls and chin-ups to build peak strength and size in your upper arms — long head, short head and brachialis.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Long Head

Beginner
  • Incline Dumbbell Curls
  • Cable Curls (Low Pulley)
  • Concentration Curls
Intermediate
  • Drag Curls
  • Spider Curls
  • Wide-Grip EZ Bar Curls
  • Scott Bench Curls
  • Strict Barbell Curls

Short Head

Beginner
  • Preacher Curls
  • Hammer Curls
  • Close-Grip EZ Bar Curls
  • High Cable Curls
  • Cross-Body Hammer Curls
Intermediate
  • Chin-Ups
  • Zottman Curls
  • Scott Bench Hammer Curls

Brachialis

Beginner
  • Hammer Curls
  • Reverse Curls
  • Cable Rope Hammer Curls
  • Plate Curls
Intermediate
  • Neutral Grip Pull-Ups
  • Reverse Preacher Curls
  • Behind-the-Back Cable Curls
