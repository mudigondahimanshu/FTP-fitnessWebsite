Arms

Forearm Breakdown

Strengthen your grip and arm endurance with wrist curls, reverse curls and farmer's carries — targeting both the flexors and extensors.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Flexors

Beginner
  • Barbell Wrist Curls
  • Dumbbell Wrist Curls
  • Reverse Cable Curls
  • Plate Pinch Holds
  • Farmer's Walks
  • Hammer Curls
  • Grip Trainer Work
Advanced
  • Towel Pull-Ups

Extensors

Beginner
  • Reverse Wrist Curls
  • Reverse Grip EZ Bar Curls
  • Finger Extensions with Bands
Intermediate
  • Wrist Rollers
  • Behind-the-Back Wrist Curls
  • Radial/Ulnar Deviations
Advanced
  • Hammer Lever Work
