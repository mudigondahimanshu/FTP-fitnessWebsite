Arms

Triceps Breakdown

Develop strong triceps with dips, pushdowns and skull crushers — essential for full upper-arm thickness across all three heads.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Long Head

Beginner
  • Overhead Triceps Extensions
  • Seated Overhead Dumbbell Extensions
  • Cable Overhead Extensions
  • Dumbbell Kickbacks
  • Rope Overhead Extensions
Intermediate
  • Skull Crushers
  • Incline EZ Bar Skull Crushers
  • Tate Press

Lateral Head

Beginner
  • Diamond Push-Ups
  • Cable Pushdowns
  • Machine Dips
  • Bench Dips
Intermediate
  • Close-Grip Bench Press
  • Parallel Bar Dips
Advanced
  • JM Press

Medial Head

Beginner
  • Triceps Pushdowns
  • Reverse Grip Pushdowns
  • Rope Pushdowns
  • V-Bar Pushdowns
  • Diamond Push-Ups
  • Close-Grip Push-Ups
Intermediate
  • Weighted Bench Dips
Advanced
  • Reverse Grip Bench Press
