Legs

Calves Breakdown

Improve lower-leg strength and endurance with calf raises and jump-based training — plus adductor and abductor work for complete lower-body balance.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Gastrocnemius

Beginner
  • Standing Calf Raises
  • Seated Calf Raises (Higher Position)
  • Jump Rope
  • Smith Machine Calf Raises
  • Leg Press Calf Raises
Intermediate
  • Donkey Calf Raises
  • Box Jumps
  • Single-Leg Calf Raises
