Legs

Legs Breakdown

Strengthen your quads, hamstrings and glutes through squats, lunges and leg presses — essential for power and balance.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Quadriceps

Beginner
  • Leg Extensions
  • Leg Press
  • Lunges
Intermediate
  • Back Squats
  • Front Squats
  • Hack Squats
  • Bulgarian Split Squats
Advanced
  • Sissy Squats

Hamstrings

Beginner
  • Leg Curls
  • Seated Leg Curls
  • Dumbbell Hamstring Curls
Intermediate
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Good Mornings
  • Swiss Ball Leg Curls
Advanced
  • Glute Ham Raises
  • Nordic Hamstring Curls

Glutes

Beginner

  • Glute Bridges
  • Cable Kickbacks
  • Frog Pumps
Intermediate
  • Bulgarian Split Squats
  • Sumo Deadlifts
  • Curtsy Lunges
  • Single-Leg Hip Thrusts
