Chest Breakdown

Build a powerful chest with presses, flyes and dips that target the upper, middle and lower pectorals — organized by difficulty so you always know where to start.

For best results, perform each exercise for 3–4 sets of 8, 10, 12, 15 or 20 reps depending on your fitness level and goals.

Upper Chest

Beginner
  • Incline Dumbbell Flyes
  • Incline Push-Ups
Intermediate
  • Incline Bench Press
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
  • Low-to-High Cable Flyes
  • Landmine Press
  • Svend Press
Advanced
  • Reverse Grip Bench Press

Middle Chest

Beginner
  • Flat Bench Press
  • Flat Dumbbell Press
  • Push-Ups
  • Machine Chest Press
  • Pec Deck Flyes
Intermediate
  • Cable Crossovers
  • Chest Dips
  • Floor Press

Lower Chest

Beginner
  • Decline Push-Ups
  • Smith Machine Decline Press
Intermediate
  • Decline Bench Press
  • Decline Dumbbell Press
  • High-to-Low Cable Flyes
  • Dips (Leaning Forward)
  • Pullovers
